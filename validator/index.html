How the RTS Validator works

This tool checks a Research Transparency Statement against the journal's policy and flags anything that needs human review.

Paste an RTS into the left panel — it validates automatically. Each section heading and each disclosure component (Conflicts of interest, Funding, Materials, etc.) gets one of three statuses:

✓ OKDisclosure statement meets default policy requirements and uses standard wording.
⚠ Needs reviewDisclosure statement does not meet default policy requirements or does not use standard wording.
✗ MissingDisclosure statement is missing.
Try the example Click Load example in the header to see the validator in action with a sample RTS that contains a mix of OK, Needs review, and Missing components.

The validation rules are loaded from rts-config.js, which is the single source of truth shared with the RTS Builder.
